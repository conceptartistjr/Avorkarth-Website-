Worldlore : Creatures / Website
-- Redesigned the creatures page to make it more visual. Each species will now have paintings as their "button", instead of simple text. Many of them still don't have descriptions, details or stats.
-- Added over twenty new artworks for the creature species.
-- Updated many creatures with TBDs, as I'm still unhappy with how stats work. Additionally, I'm working on an overhaul of the base systems of Avorkarth, to continue to make it more easily playable and readable while keeping its complexity. Rulebook
-- Updated how Toughness works. Instead of being a number (that will stay for armours and natural defenses like scales), it's simply a size thing. Creatures can deal normal damage to all creatures their own size or one larger. Otherwise, the damage is reduced by 1/10th per size category.
-- Wounds have been changed to accomodate how the toughness change is, so now wounds are categorized.
