-Setting-

"The cliff-ridden pass of Radell has often been the bane of many travelling merchants, for within the rocky caves that line the pathway are said to be the homes of dark creatures who prey upon the unsuspecting..."

"You find yourself upon an old cobbled road, long since overgrown with weeds and the fallen dirt from the surrounding small cliffsides. It is nearing night of a fresh autmunal day, the moon lazily beaming through the occasional cloud from above within an otherwise clear blue sky. Turning the next corner as you keep heading north, you see a rather large clearing up ahead, with a campfire keeping company to a man, a horse and a small cart..."
