LEGIONS OF ARENYA

HUMANS : THE SIX REALMS OF HUMANITY
KARTHIAN KINGDOMS Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 9%
The mighty human realm of kings, queens and knights, the karthians are the proud rulers of the mid-lands of Avorkarth, and by far its most spread populace.
SHARASSIAN DUNE-KINGS Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 7%
A great human peoples who have grown in the harsh dunes of the Sharassian deserts and have made it their own claim with trade and wonders.
THRAKAEN CLANS Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 6%
The towering humans of the north, the Thrakaen clans are a proud peoples who view personal greatness and legends as their driving goal often leading to mighty expeditions from their wintry shores and lands.
XIA EMPIRE Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 5%
The philosophical humans of the east, the Xia grew in islation alongside their far-off lands of a thousand mysteries and have since harnessed that curiosity for tales and stories as power.
TILDRAN WARLORDS Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 5%
The blood-witch and warlord clans of the Tildran peoples once ruled the west, and though have been enslaved and pushed back by the Karthians and in-fighting. While looked down as a slave class in karthia while captured, they would still bring terror should the free clans unite once more.
DAELDEN KINGDOM Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 2%
Humanity's finest, the Daelden are the remnants of elder humans from the line of the first kings and queens and who are blessed with longer life and greater wisdom.

RUNNOC : SONS OF THE MOUNTAINS
THE GREAT MOUNTAIN HALLS Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 3%
The deep dwarves, miners and mighty warriors of great renown, their crafting with stone, metal and rock is legendary and with it a knowledge they keep to themselves of a runic language that can make stone move on its own.

AELVENKIN : THE CRYSTALLINE EMPIRES
SANGUINNAR HIGHLORDS Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 1%
The followers of the last elven high king before the fall, immortal be his soul, the Sanguinnar live in the golden glades and protect their last shimmering crystal cities with a xenophobic fierceness, for within the borders are lies and blood and shadow, the secrets to their longevity and power.
SILDAEN COUNCIL Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 0.5%
The elven populace who followed the prophet and high-priest Eloran at the end of the fourth age, who warned that the Sanguinnar faction's want of power would destroy the Aelvenkin empire. Their silver and crystal cities are the last remnants of a forgotten age.

AELVENKIN : ELVEN OUTCASTS
NAREI COUNCIL Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 1%
At the end of the fourth age, the Sanguinnar had even enslaved their own people. The Narei are the aelvenkin who rebelled and escaped their masters, left behind their crystalline ancestry and decided to return to a more natural life style.

FALGRII : THE HILL FOLK
FALGRII LITTLE FOLK Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 3%
The falgrii aren't much of a fighting force, their power lying in creating good food and drink and having a relaxing pipe upon green hills. Nonetheless, they will defend their lands and that of their allies.

ANGELS : KEEPERS OF THE LIGHT
ANGELIC FORCES Land in dominion : TBD% Population : -Summon only-
While lesser angelic beings are rare enough to appear upon the land of creation, should an angel actually be summoned by Arenya's children, divine retribution is sure to follow.

REACH OF ATRONACH

GURAHZ : HORDES OF KORRAGEN
ORCISH HORDES Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 5%
The warring vicious orcs, lesser and greater alike, all follow the original will of their creator and blood-god, Korragen, which is to spread a land of smoke, dust and fire upon the realm of creation.
GOBLINOID INFESTATIONS Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 7%
Within any cavernous or rocky area, one is sure to find a goblinoid village spreading and infesting the area, for they are an insatiable race of resource wasters who constantly need more and more.

DAEMONS : THE FIVE HALLS
HALLS OF PRIDE Land in dominion : TBD% Population : -Summon only-
It is said that every other Hall of Atronach is bound to the halls of pride, and that they are the masters of the Halls. Even a lesser daemon of pride called upon the mortal plane beckons the end of a kingdom.
HALLS OF SORROW Land in dominion : TBD% Population : -Summon only-
Manipulators and masters of plots, the Daemons of the Halls of Sorrow find great joy in creating webs of intruigue and deception, only for the mortals who follow the thread to have their soul eaten.
HALLS OF WRATH Land in dominion : TBD% Population : -Summon only-
The warring Daemons of Wrath, they ever seek bloodshed and violence, their hatred for all things guiding their weapons against anything in their path to a destroyed and broken realm.
HALLS OF LUST Land in dominion : TBD% Population : -Summon only-
The Daemons of the Halls of Lust seek hedonistic pleasure, mixed with an insatiable delight for torture, pain and emotional wrack. Their beauty is matched by none, but so is the lack of interest in anything but their own sexual need.
HALLS OF GREED Land in dominion : TBD% Population : -Summon only-
The Daemons of Greed are small and weak compared to all other Halls, although still incredibly mighty to most mortals. Their impish grins are wide and inviting as wealth and power is dangled before their unsuspecting victims.

LAZRAS' TREATY

TAERUK : DEVOTED OF THE MARSHES AND JUNGLES
LAZRANIIR Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 4%
The blood thirsty collective of the Lazraniir are the most prideful and strongest of the Taeruk, for their four-armed humanoid shape and skin is that of Lazras himself, the reptillian Lizard.
SZRASSAS Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 3%
The Szrassas collective are a grouping of snake-like humanoids from the torso up, their heads and fangs and beasts and even cities mimicking the image of great snakes.
VENAAKU Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 2%
The Venaaku are smaller toad like humanoids, their high-priests and warriors masters of poisons and venoms, their society following the same venomous traits of treachery and backstabbing.
KRAKAANASS Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 2%
The hulking Krakaanass are mixtures of humanoid bodies with the maws, scales and tails of a crocodile. Their ability to stalk prey is incredible, able to stop even the most miniscule of movements from their bodies for hours on end.
KIIRASSZA / ARACHS Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 4%
The fallen bat Taeruk, the Kiirassza renounced their bat-like god when they pledged themselves as slaves to the once powerful Arachnea. As such they were cursed, their humanoid body distorting and heads becoming that of spiders, but their arms still with useless leathery "wings".

CREATURES OF YSHAE AND SYLHAEL

FAE-FOLK : NATURE'S KEEPERS
NYMPHIC GUARDIANS Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 0.1%
The first of the fae, the excrutiaingly beautiful Sei'yuulei dwell and guard their remaining leafy stone and silver glades fiercely, protectors and keepers of nature. They sacrificed themselves to seal the great darkness long ago and are now too few to hold off the squabbling younger races from spreading and consuming the realm.
SMALL FEY-FOLKEN Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 0.5%
Pixies, gnomes and other small fey-folk all gather together in open glades and fields where they live within their mushroom and flower huts. While not really fighters, they will bring their tickling and sneezing spells to aid their nymphic allies when needed.
LIVING FORESTS Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 0.2%
The older ancient living trees of Kerethar are the guardians and councillors of the fae, guiding them in their troubles and giving them shade to rest when needed. Their sons, the treants and grand-daughters, the dryads, aid the fae in the protection and caretaking of the forests.

ANCIENT CREATURES : NATURE'S WRATH
ANIMAL PREDATORS : NATURE'S CYCLE Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 9%
The animals of nature, they know little of the workings of the world other than their life cycle, but will heed the call of the fae, friend or masters when needed.
GREATER BEASTS Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 0.1%
From hydras and dragons to behemoths and leviathans and all other nearly extinct creatures, the great beasts of the early ages are mighty and imposing, each a force to be reckoned with.

ELEMENTAL PACTS : ANCIENT ALLIANCE
PACT OF THE SEA Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 0.5%
Those who were made of both the mortal and elemental realm of of water who aided in the second and third age against the darkness such as the thresher sirens and merfolk.
PACT OF WINDS Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 0.5%
Those who were made of both the mortal and elemental realm of air who aided in the second and third age against the darkness such as the syllan harpies, couatles and lillends.
PACT OF THE EARTH Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 0.5%
Those who were made of both the mortal and elemental realm of earth who aided in the second and third age against the darkness such as the grolnar and goragh.
PACT OF FLAMES Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 0.5%
Those who were made of both the mortal and elemental realm of fire who aided in the second and third age against the darkness such as the salamanders and phoenixes.

LAND GODS : NATURE'S SPIRIT GUARDIANS
SPIRIT BEASTS Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 0.01%
The mystical and starlight-like great spirit beasts of Anaeila watch and guide the Sei'yuulei as well as those bound to the ancient forests.
SPIRITS OF JIAN : KI'TZAI Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 0.05%
The benevolant spirits Jian, land of a thousand mysteries, are as mysterious as their origin. Ranging from mighty land gods to lesser spirits of luck, they look after the creatures of Jian.
SPIRITS OF JIAN : KI'TZAU Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 0.05%
The evil spirits Jian, land of a thousand mysteries, ever seek to engorge themselves upon the creatures of Jian, and are often born from a mixture of a mortal or ki'tzai host, and a negative event that affected the realm. They constantly hunger for more spirit and blood.

ELEMENTAL CONCLAVES : PRIMAL FORCES

ELEMENTAL CONCLAVES : PRIMAL FORCES
WATER ELEMENTALS : YUL'TAE'S REALM Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 0.2%
The calmest of the elementals, those from the realm of waters will more often than not be either passive or aid those nearby... unless one taints their lakes, rivers and streams.
AIR ELEMENTALS : SEL'TYA'S REALM Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 0.2%
The elementals from the realm of air are aloof, yet tempestous, their motives quickly changing from one to the other without warning.
EARTH ELEMENTALS : GOR'KAN'S REALM Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 0.2%
The elementals of earth are the most distant of the elementals, often leaving alone and wishing to be left alone. As long as others do not tread on their grounds, they will continue their earthen toil.
FIRE ELEMENTALS : FEO'RAG'S REALM Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 0.2%
The elementals from the realm of fire are vicious and destructive, their lashing anger always wanting to find more fuel for their realm.

ELDRITCH ENTITIES

UNDEAD : HORDES OF UNDEATH
UNDEAD ARMIES Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 4%
Undying and unstoppable, the forces of undeath cannot be vanquished in combat, only their march to death temporarily halted. It is their source, the great darkness of the second age, that whispers to powerful spellcasting mortals the secrets of necromancy, and ever spread its power.

UNDEAD : NIGHT FEEDERS
VAMPIRIC CHILDREN Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 2%
A newer form of undeath recently discovered, the crimson council rose from the whispers of the great darkness and have since spread their curse throughout the realm.

IKKANI : ANCIENT EMPIRES
EMPIRE OF NAQU'KARIR Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 0.1%
Once the greatest empire of the south for hundreds of thousands of years, the insectoid Ikkani were incredibly scientifically advanced, fascinated with biological mutations and the greatest of necromantic achievers. After a great war with the Sei'yuulei, they were made dormant and will hopefully never return. The Naqu'karir serve the Voiceless ones.
EMPIRE OF IQUENARI Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 0.1%
The Naqu'karir serve the Sightless ones.
EMPIRE OF VIEK'QNI Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 0.1%
The Viek'qni serve the Mindless ones.

ANCIENT TERRORS : CREATURES OF THE VOID
THE VOICELESS Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 0.01%
Never should the voice escape, for it will be stolen.
THE SIGHTLESS Land in dominion : TBD% Population : -Summon Only-
Never should the sight blink, for it will be taken.
THE MINDLESS Land in dominion : TBD% Population : -Summon Only-

DREAMS OF TILVAER

MECHANICALS : THE SILVER SPIRES
OBSIDIUM FORGES Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 0.1%
The obsidium forges are the most durable of the mechanical beings created by Tilvaer. Theirs is the role to guard the silver spires and are the last defense to its core.
STEELSCALE FORGES Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 0.2%
The most common and adaptable of the forges, the steelscale forges creates mechanical beings made as the vanguard and frontline of the silver spires' forces.
QUICKSILVER FORGES Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 0.1%
The quicksilver forges are flexible and shimmering. Theirs is the essence of flexibility and focus, their blades sharp and their weapons specialized to every need.
OVERSEERS Land in dominion : TBD% Population : 0.01%
The overseers are the minds left behind by Tilvaer to watch and keep the silver spires running optimally. They watch and operate the forges as necessary.

FACTION RELATIONS AND ALLIANCES

Kindred : The invading faction will not turn upon the defending faction even should no other threat be present. Peace reigns in areas with only these factions.

Allied : The invading faction will rarely turn upon the defending faction. Peace between the factions is mostly held, other than radical subfactions or surfacings of greed for land or power.

Unease : The invading faction is open to trade and will assist the defending faction in times of need. The faction is wary of any of the other faction upon their territory, often looking at them with either fear or contempt.

Hostile : The invading faction will attack the defending faction on sight, with very few rare exceptions. The factions will very rarely ally, usually only if there is a greater threat to both sides.

Hated : The invading faction will look out for the defending faction to hunt them down, but not with a priority over current wars and greater threats. The factions will only ally if there is a greater threat to both sides.

Wrathful : The invading faction will openly seek out the defending faction, often raising armies to attempt to destroy the other. The factions will not ally under any circumstance without another bridging faction present.

Lordship : The invading faction is master of the defending faction, and rules over them without question.

Servitude : The invading faction is subserviant of the defending faction, and obeys without question.
