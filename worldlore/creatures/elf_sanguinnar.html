AELVENKIN - SANGUINNAR

MEDIUM HUMANOID

The eldest remaining civilization of the fourth age, the aelvenkin, or elves as named by humanity, had discovered sun based arcanic manipulation and harnessed its secrets to create their rule. This age ended after thousands of years of opulence when they failed to stop their own insane lust for power and the unfortunate mastery of blood magic. The revolt of the lower class along with the greatest invasion of the southern dark orcs tribes of the age split their species thin and led to the destruction of most of their ancient civilization.

The tall, fair and elegant aelvenkin are the visual representation of immortality. Powered by their cystalline implant, the unaging fair-folk of the golden lands used the ancient discovery of crystalline arcanic magics to make their lives one of opulence and decadence. Still millenia after the fall of the aelvenkin empire, their last remaining cities of gold and crystal seem nothing short of invincible, the secrets of their powers having long been kept to themselves.

SANGUINNAR CULTURE

The last remaining cities of the Sanguinnar are still those of opulence and decadence. They mostly ignore the outside world, their high priests clouding their minds with the lies that all is still well, and that their immortality is given freely. Most Sanguinnars know where the flow of endless blood comes from, but their culture views all others as necessary sacrifices and is reflected in their arrogant demeanors. While a few Sanguinnars have since come to like their less advanced allies, some even forgiving the Narei and Sildaen treachery, most lie upon the dreams of their once great empire and will do anything, including sacrificing allies, simply so that they may live on and be one of the chosen upon the Eternal King's return.

SANGUINNAR RELIGION

The Sanguinnar's belief is centered around the return of the Eternal King. Their newly arisen God had, at the height of his power ages ago, paved the way for their glorious empire upon golden lands, removing the lesser human and orcish kind who had begun to encroach. The Eternal King gave them immortality and power in the form of crystal and left secrets in the form of blood for those who had the willpower to do so. The Sanguinnar believe it is their houses' rule to make the return of the Eternal King, even at the cost of their own kind.

SANGUINNAR DIMORPHISMS

The male and female of the aelvenkin species are similar to humanity in their differences, although to a greater extent. Males are of stronger build by nature, while the female is much more slender and graceful. Beyond this, they are very much alike in their mind and abilities.
