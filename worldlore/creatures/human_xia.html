HUMAN - XIA

MEDIUM HUMANOID

Humans are not the eldest, nor the strongest, nor the most agile of races, yet they are the most common upon the continents. This is due to the fact that humans are flexibly adaptive to most environments and opportunities, harshly judge those that are not alike and spread really, really quickly. Add to that a suprisingly high potential for great minds, and humans have risen to create a dominant civilization upon the central lands of the realm. Humanity has survived many ages but their history has often been rewritten, destroyed and simply forgotten due to their warlike tendencies. Since their age dawned many millenia ago, humanity spread and split into many variants, each evolving depending on their settled area.

In the far eastern misted lands of Jian, the human variation known as Xia take residence in philosophical harmony. Xia are known for their reverence of respect, beauty and above all, balance in all things, as taught to them millenia ago by the being known as the First Heavenly Emperor. It is said that if one observes a Xia, they can feel a strange aura of life and purpose in their every action. The Xia will respond that it is their way to respect ki, the force that lives in all things and makes them whole.

XIA CULTURE

Xia's culture is very much an odd one to most other human variations as it hinges mostly on philosophy and wonder. Questions asked can go through debates for centuries before simply being left alone, becoming another of the thousand mysteries of Jian. The Xia's reverence of respect is also obvious as even the most hated of Xia brethren will often speak to one another before they act upon the more primal instincts of warfare. This makes it very strange for the more straightforward races to live alongside the philosophical Xia, their direct questions toyed with and tossed around only for a hundred different answers to fall from the heavens above, each a plausible solution.

XIA RELIGION

The Xia religion is the worship of the spirits of the land. These can manifest in just about anything, and almost everything has a spirit associated with it. Xia therefore mostly pray and give offerings to the spirit that watches over their part of the land, known as a Land God. Spirits however, are not all benevolant. The Xia therefore seprerate spirits into two categories, the Ki'tzai (Kind Spirit) that watch over humanity and nature, and the Ki'tzau (Evil Spirit), known to eat human souls and care only for themselves. Yet above all Land Gods, spirits and men, there are supposedly five great Spirit Gods, watching the very balance of Jian itself and each taught their ways by the august First Heavenly Emperor, breather of life.

XIA DIMORPHISMS

The male and female of the human species has very slight differences, other than their visual distinctions. Males are of slightly stronger build by nature, while the female tends to be more slender and graceful, although both are able to ascend and surpass one another in these regards. Beyond this, they are very much alike in the mind and abilities.
