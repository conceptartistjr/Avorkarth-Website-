GNOLL

MEDIUM HUMANOID

Hunched, furred, and toothy, gnolls are hyena-like humanoids sharing the hyena's distinctive "laughing" and scavenging habits. Bloodthirsty, vicious and very aggressive creatures, gnolls are easily angered and always hungry, making very poor creatures of diplomacy.

GNOLL CULTURE

Gnolls live in tribal, extremely patriarchal, "villages" of stolen, ripped tarps and cloths. While they do not know how, nor have the intelligence nor dexterity to weave cloth, their desires for such living styles alongside their vicious nature makes the gnoll culture a raiding one. Caravans and travelling merchants of all species are the lifeblood and sustenance of gnoll culture, for otherwise they simply hunt, mate and reproduce. Gnoll raiders will kill males on sight, and enjoy females of common humanoid origins before slaughtering them too. On occaisions of low resources, gnolls will amass and pillage the nearby villages instead. Equally if a larger predator is hunting within their territory, gnolls will gladly simply take the remains of its hunt as prizes.

GNOLL RELIGION

Gnolls have no exact religious figure that watches over them, instead following the ramblings of their shamanistic fortune-tellers. The spiritual leaders will often crush many natural herbs and throw them into the fire and breathe the smoke. This will lead to visions from what they believe to be the spirits living beyond all things. However, most gnolls are bloodthirsty and will only follow the ramblings speaking of conquest and victory.

GNOLL DIMORPHISMS

Gnolls have a shared look by the males and females, with very little difference between the two other than the large seperation of size and muscle mass. Their intelligence is equivalent to one another, but nontheless very poor.
