ORC - GURAHZ

MEDIUM HUMANOID

Orcs, or gurahz in the orcish tongue, are grey-brown skinned humanoids of medium size. Slightly larger and more muscled than the average karthian human, orcs are also much more short lived and prone to problematic body disfigurements and diseases. This makes orcs quite visually distinctive from one another, while keeping to an overall recognizable form.

ORC CULTURE

Orcs are a theoretically civilized entirely patriarchal culture, with blackened metallic cities of spires and spikes. They function upon the basis of power, ruled by very short reigned overlords and chieftains who have murdered, betrayed or simply killed those in their way to attain that position. Orcs, when not vying for their own personal power, are prone to march out and attack any other nearby civilization due to their incredible bloodlust and the need of fresh land, which they raze and destroy at an incredible rate. Male orcs are also known to capture females of more attractive species, as their own lust is often not sated by the lesser beauty of their own females.

ORC RELIGION

Orcs follow a singluar god, their creator, Korahg'gan, he who is blood, shadow and flame. While shrines and statues to Korr'aggan are very rare, the orcs' zeal and servitude towards their god is ever-lasting and they are known to chant guttaral screams to their bloody god during combat, or while rippping apart a corpse of a strong victim in order to eat their innards, believing this will grant them the other's strength.

ORC DIMORPHISMS

The male and female of the orc species are, much like humanity, different in build and shape, but to a much larger extent. Males are of stronger build and larger frame, as well as being taller by nature, while females are much smaller, frailer and weaker. Beyond this, they are very much alike in their mind and abilities.
