GORAGH

LARGE BEAST

Many rocky hills within the realm host terrible beasts known as goragh. Often mistaken for an inert hill or large rock, the goragh are large brown-grey skinned creatures with a sharp toothed red bill and beak and a shell-like back fused to their skin which resembles and is just as durable as a gathering of rocks. Their arms are thick and muscled with massive open "hands" tipped by sharp curled claws made for digging.

GORAGH CULTURE

Goraghs are creatures with beastial intelligence, and therefore do not have a culture per say. They live as gatherings in cliffs and rocky valleys, digging into the ground and nestling into the ground to pose as rocks. There they wait for prey to wander nearby, mistaking them to be part of the environment. However, goraghs are also naturally slow and lazy, and will often fall into a slumber that may last a very long time, effectively becoming part of the landscape. Should a prey escape their initial attack, they will not chase, and instead go back into the ground.

GORAGH RELIGION

Goragh are beasts of nature and therefore have no religion, but will respect faeic creatures as well as the greater powers and spirits of nature.

GORAGH DIMORPHISMS

The male and female of the goragh species are extremely similar, and the only difference is that of genitalia.
