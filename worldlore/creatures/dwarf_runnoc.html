DWARF - RUNNOC

MEDIUM HUMANOID

The mighty sons and daughters of the mountain halls are an honorable race, their runebound laws etched into their every action. Runnoc are known to be trustworthy adventuring allies whose toughness equates to their rocky homes and who are master stoneworkers. Although of a shorter stature, their bulkiness, muscles and fiery spirit make up for it in terms of combat expertise. However, one should be wary of getting into an argument with a dwarf, as their stubbornness is legendary.

RUNNOC CULTURE

Runnoc law is absolute. There is no deviation, there is no faltering, the roots of the mountains is where the written word is unwavering. Runnoc culture is centered around their language, which almost only deals with absolutes much like mathematical equations. As such, their civilizations are incredibly stubborn, but very fair. Should the word of the law favor one, it will do so without wavering, only ever changing should another truth be unveiled. However, while their laws and rules may seem slightly extreme to any outsider, it does not mean that they do not enjoy life. The Runnoc's liking of drinks and foods rivals that of the Falgrii and their deep stone halls bear witness to bountiful feasts by roaring fires.

RUNNOC RELIGION

While the statues lining the halls of the Runnoc are given to their Kings of old and the memories of the greatest smiths and rulers is prevalent in the Runnoc culture, their worship is given to a singular God, no matter the mountain hall. Volkayn, the greatest smith, the father of all dwarven kin, he who shaped the primal world into the mountains and valleys which house the Runnoc. Entire sub-sections of the mountain caves, often those with the most precious metals and gems, is given to monumental representations of Volkayn and his immortal sons. However, ever since their trade with their above-ground neighbours, the most common religion of Eonna has also found its way into the minds of a few Runnoc.

RUNNOC DIMORPHISMS

The male and female of the runnoc species are similar to humanity in their differences. Males have a more chiseled build by nature, while the female are more rounded and softer shaped. Beyond this, they are very much alike in their mind and abilities.
