OGRE

LARGE HUMANOID

The hulking humanoid creatures known as ogres are cave dwellers who often venture out from deep below in search of prey and food. Larger framed than humans in relation to their size, ogres are creatures of muscle, attacking prey head-on and through brute force instead of using intruigue or stealth.

OGRE CULTURE

Ogres are mostly singular beings, often a male with two or three mating females within a caved area. Extremely territorial and possessive, ogres will mark their territory simply by the sheer amount of kills and mutilated bodies, leaving corpses half-ravaged and eaten. With little intelligence, their language seems to be mostly grunts and huffs. Yet it has been remarked upon that ogres at least have the intelligence of cooperating and banding together when a larger predator threatens their area. This does not last long usually, and most often they will turn upon one another. Ogres have also been known to be enslaved as the muscle for goblins and orcs, if kept well fed or chained, and allowed to rampage from time to time.

OGRE RELIGION

Ogres have barely even a shamanistic belief and will only sometimes be seen acting out very crude religious ceremonies. Already mostly individualistic beings and rather dim, ogres have little need for religion of any sort beyond blood-based feasts. The few rituals seen and documented have always been related to bone castings for a good hunt or finding food.

OGRE DIMORPHISMS

The male and female of the ogre species are similar to humanity in their differences, although to a greater extent. Males are of stronger and larger build, while females are thinner and smaller. Beyond this, they are very much alike in their mind and abilities.
