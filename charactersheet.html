CHARACTER SHEET

Steps to copy a sheet

1A. Click on the button. It should create an editable copy. If not, follow from 1B instead.

1B. Open the original example sheet with the button above. (Right-click the button and choose open in new tab).

2. Click on "File", then "Make a Copy".

3. Open the Copy in your google drive/sheets and edit to your hearts desire.

4. (Optional) Remove all current texts and save as a pdf version and/or print out a physical copy.
